Atti Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/12/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Atti, a social media app designed to connect people, share experiences, and build communities. At Atti, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Atti app.

2. Information We Collect

a. Account Information:

When you sign up for Atti, we collect your username, email address, and password to create and secure your account.

b. Profile Information:

You may choose to provide additional information such as your name, profile picture, bio, and other details to personalize your Atti profile.

c. Usage Data:

We collect data on how you interact with the app, including posts, comments, likes, and the content you view.

d. Device Information:

We gather information about the device you use to access Atti, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

e. Location Data:

If you enable location services, we collect your device's location to enhance your experience and provide location-based features.

f. Contacts:

With your permission, we may access your device's contacts to help you connect with friends on Atti.

3. How We Use Your Information

a. Personalization:

We use your information to personalize your Atti experience, including suggesting connections, content, and features.

b. Communication:

We may use your email address to send you important updates, notifications, and promotional materials related to Atti.

c. Analytics:

Aggregate and anonymized data is used for analytics to improve the app's performance, features, and user experience.

d. Security:

Your information is used to secure your account and protect against unauthorized access.

4. Sharing Your Information

a. Public Content:

Information you share publicly on Atti, such as posts and comments, may be visible to other users.

b. Third-Party Services:

We may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. Your information may be shared with these services in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. Your Choices and Controls

a. Account Settings:

You can update and manage your account settings, including privacy preferences, in the Atti app.

b. Communication Preferences:

You have the option to control the types of communications you receive from Atti.

c. Data Access and Deletion:

You can request access to your personal data or request its deletion by contacting our support team.

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

7. Children's Privacy

Atti is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to delete it.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and services. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@atti.app.

Thank you for trusting Atti with your social experience!
